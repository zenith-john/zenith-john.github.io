Research Interset

My research interest lies in enumerative geometry and mathematical physics, especially about Donaldson-Thomas type invariants. These invariants are at the interface of many areas, including mirror symmetry, integrable system, geometric Langlands, Bridgeland stability, low-dimensional topology and supersymmetric gauge theory. Occasionally, I think about transporting geometric ideas to number theory via (enhanced) Weil’s Rosetta stone. For details, see here.
